  • Robin Hartshorne, Algebraic Geometry (Chapter III: Cohomology)

Mark Breakdown

Component Percentage
Assignments (graded on completion) 40%

Assignments

Assignments will be exercises from Hartshorne (and external sources), graded on completeness, not correctness.

Term Test

There will be one term test on February 24, the entire class period.

Final Assessment

The final assessment will be held during the last week of class.
